A Home Away From Home

TANIORE INN

'Tanjore Inn' is curated to offer a memorable experience. All its air conditioned rooms, equipped with modern facilities and furnished with unique antique pieces present an agrecable ambience, Our Library contains rare books which are available for reading on request. It has a well equipped kitchen that provides homely food. Its care take services, CCTV's and round the clock security services are at your beck and call. Special functions, guided tours, art and cultural programs are offered on special request. Our travel desk takes care of transport arrangements at discounted rates.

Apart from its speculating amenities, it is situated with a magnificent view of the world famous Rajarajesvaram, or "The Big Temple' - from the terrace of Tanjore Inn. Also, renowned Maharaja Serfoji II Saraswathi Mahal Library, the enchanting city of Thanjavur and the Railhead, Bus stand are all at walkable distance.
